👩🏻‍💻 I am Eleonora and I am a PostDoc at Istituto Dalle Molle di Studi sull'Intelligenza Artificiale (IDSIA), USI - SUPSI.

✍🏻 I am part in the OC Group, lead by Luca Maria Gambardella and Monaldo Mastrolilli. My interests involve theoretical an practical hardness of combinatorial optimization problems.
