About Me

Who Am I?

I, am Eleonora and I am a PostDoc at Istituto Dalle Molle di Studi sull'Intelligenza Artificiale (IDSIA), USI - SUPSI.

I work in the research group of Luca Maria Gambardella and Monaldo Mastrolilli.

My interests involve theoretical an practical hardness of combinatorial optimization problems.
